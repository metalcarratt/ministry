# The ministry of the Word and God's dispensing for God's economy

## Message One - "Steadfastly in Prayer and in the Ministry of the Word"

### Genuine prayer - joining with the Spirit
- a man of prayer is one who seeks God and God's will.
- we are nothing and can do nothing
- when we pray we are seeking God. God is not happy when out attitude is, "I know everything."
- "Lord, what is in Your heart that You want me to pray about?" **
- "Genuine prayers" - prayers that are mingled with God the Spirit. There are prayers that are not prayers. Prayer must be a joint prayer in which God is mingled with our spirit.

James 5:17
> Elijah was a man of like feeling with us, and he earnestly prayed that it would not rain; and it did not rain on the earth for three years and six months.

Lit. "prayed in a prayer"

- We and the Holy Spirit pray together in the fellowship of the two spirits.

### The prayer of authority
- We first need the preceeding point, to empty ourselves of our will, to absorb God and His will
- We should believe that we *have* receied, not that we *will* receive.
