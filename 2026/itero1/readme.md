# The ministry of the Word and God's dispensing for God's economy

## Message One - "Steadfastly in Prayer and in the Ministry of the Word"

### Genuine prayer - joining with the Spirit
- a man of prayer is one who seeks God and God's will.
- we are nothing and can do nothing
- when we pray we are seeking God. God is not happy when out attitude is, "I know everything."
- "Lord, what is in Your heart that You want me to pray about?" **
- "Genuine prayers" - prayers that are mingled with God the Spirit. There are prayers that are not prayers. Prayer must be a joint prayer in which God is mingled with our spirit.

James 5:17
> Elijah was a man of like feeling with us, and he earnestly prayed that it would not rain; and it did not rain on the earth for three years and six months.

Lit. "prayed in a prayer"

- We and the Holy Spirit pray together in the fellowship of the two spirits.

### The prayer of authority
- We first need the preceeding point, to empty ourselves of our will, to absorb God and His will
- We should believe that we *have* receied, not that we *will* receive.
- Book: *Lessons on Prayer*, Chapter: *Prayer and Warfare" - Prayer is in the heavenly realm. It is the driving out of Satan and the ushering in of God's kingdom. These are prayers of ascension, prayers of warfare.
- Facts:
  - Christ has already conquered the enemy.
  - Apply His victory.
  - We are sitting with Him in ascension.
  - We are higher than all the evil powers in the air.
  - Daniel prayed for three weeks before (until) the will of God reached the earth through the angel.
- Faith: not only believing God can or will but that God has done already
- Apply God's authority to deal with problems and things that ought to be removed.
  - praying, giving a mesage, shepherding, should all be done under authority.
- Command what God has commanded and give orders to what He has given orders to.
- The church can have such prayer of authority by having *full faith*, being without doubt, being clear that **what we do is fully according to God's will**
  - Q: What is "full" faith?
  - Matt. 6:10, Your kingdom come
  - Matt. 18:19-20, In heaven or on earth
- every overcomer must learn to "speak to this mountain"

Many saints are living and functioning according to God's permissive will, not His perfect will. In this age of grace the Lord allows. But what about at the judgment seat? If we follow the Lord in His perfect will, what grace will follow!

### The ministry of the Word

- every verse can be unpacked in a wonderful way
  - it's not just "a verse"
  - example: Eph. 3:17a, "that Christ may make His home in your hearts" - this verse will become an entire conference.
- our memory
  - the Lord wants us to remember wonderful things, glorious things, not things of shame. The Lord has dealt with our past.
  - by the cross slay the demons who bring up memories
- The only way for Him to become our person is to make His home in our hearts
- Strengthen the spirit, make it the strongest part.
- The breadth, length, height and depth
  - An aspect of the Body
  - A sequence. We don't come to the depths straight away. Breadth: love all the saints. Then length. Height: ascension.
  - A cycle, even every day, breadth, length, height, depth.
  - experiences like these are *Body matters*. When we go to this realm and go higher and higher the enemy knows and attacks.
    - **A BODY MATTER**
    - If we are not in the Body our height will attract the enemy's attack. We need to be in the Body so that we can reach the height, and even further, the depth.
