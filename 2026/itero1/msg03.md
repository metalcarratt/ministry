# Message Three - Ministering Life for the Body

It's not about teaching exposition, it's ministering CHrist as life to man. Have the goal of the building up of the Body.
How do you feel about the churches we are caring for? Are our churches in the fulness of life?
What is our stature? Still babyish? Still quaralling with one another? Or are we a full-grown man? We all sense the closeness of the Lord's return,
but there is a crucial need of life.

The root problem in Corinth was division that caused all the other problems. But the root of division was the shortage of life.
Paul said, You are childish, babyish. The Lord knows the condition of our church. He has the seven eyes working in the midst of the seven lampstands.
The base issue is we are short of life. We have activity, we have knowledge, but we are short of life.
We are in a warfare. Fighting to finish the building work. How to have, how to handle, ho to minister this life to one another.
