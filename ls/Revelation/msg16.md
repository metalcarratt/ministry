laodecia

the speaker:
- the amen (firm, steadfaat, trustworthy)
- the faithful and true witness
- the beginning of the creation (the source of God's work)

the church:
- neither hot nor cols but lukewarm
- proud of being rich
- wretched, miserable, poor, blind, naked
- about to be vommited out of the Lord's mouth
- the Lord standing at the door and knocking

buying gold refined by fire. gold signifies the divine nature of God, and the faith through which we can participate in the divine nature. in order to gain this we need to pay a price. if we ask the Lord for this living faith He may ask us to drop something. that is the price. if we are willing to drop it we will gain the gold. Paul said He counted all things loss that He might gain Christ.
