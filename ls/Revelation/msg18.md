ch 5 another scene in heaven.

a scroll in the right hand of Him who
sits up in the throne. this scroll
contains the meaning of the universe, 
the secret of God's economy. this
mystery is unsealed by the ascended
Christ. 

the worthy One - the Lion Lamb. To
the enemy He is the Lion, to John He
was the Lamb. He was freshly slain,
indicating this scene is just after
His ascension. 

He is the root and branch of David. 

As the Lion He has overcome Satan and
as the Lamb He has taken away our sin. 
Thus He has overcome and is worthy to
open this scroll. 

seven horns for fighting the enemy. 

seven eyes which are the seven Spirits
of God, searching and burning. In
Zechariah He is the stone with seven
eyes, and these eyes are the seven lamps. the seven lamps are something
of the building, being in the tabernacle. thus this searching and
burning Spirit is not just for judging
but for building. Even the whole book
of revelation is ultimately a book on
building, with the new Jerusalem at
the end. God's judging is His
procedure to gain the building.
