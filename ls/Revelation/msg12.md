#### the hidden manna
> Some of us have had this experience of the hidden Christ. We have said, "Lord, I don't care for the world. I only care for You, Lord, not any human relationship or friendship. Lord, I am willing to drop 0every tie. Lord, now I'm thoroughly free and I love You from the depths of my being. I love You without anything frustrating me." When we say this to the Lord, we are immediately in the golden pot, in the intimacy of the divine nature, partaking of the hidden Christ. Oh, we must eat such a Christ.

#### the white stone with the new name

> Every transformed believer as a "white stone" bears "a new name...which no one knows but he who receives it." Such a "new name" is the interpretation of the experience of the one being transformed. Hence only he himself knows the meaning of that name.

> This new name is simply the designation of what this brother is. Since this new name is based upon what this brither is according to his experiences, others cannot know what it is.
