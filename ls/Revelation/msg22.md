many christians know to praise the lamb as worthy because of redemption,
but not many realise we need to praise Him as worthy because He has overcome
the enemy, accomplished redemption, and been qualified to open tbe scroll
of God's economy.

there are seven lamp, seven eyes and seven Spirits, which are all one.
the seven lamps are first mentioned in exodus, but at that point it is
not clear what they mean. in zechariah we see seven lamps, seven eyes
and the Spirit. but in revelation we see they are all one.

the seven lamps are for god's building. the seven Spirits as the seven eyes of 
the lamb in revelation are the seven eyes of the stone for the rebuilding of
the temple, God's house.

God builds by the seven eyes. the eyes are for judging, purifying and infusing.
by this infusing, we become the same as God to be His building.
