Christ is "another Angel". In His administration of God's judgment on the earth,
He comes as another Angel, a special Angel.

Christ appears at the first altar, the altar of burnt offering. He takes the
golden censor (the saints prayers) and adds to it incense (His merit and
achievements that makes our prayer acceptable to God) and offers it on the
golden incense altar to God.

the answer to this prayer is fire taken from the altar are cast to the earth,
causing thunders, voices, lightning, an earthquake. this indicates, first, that
the saints prayer here must be for God's judment on the earth for it's opposition
to His economy. at that time the situation on the earth will be so severe His
saints cannot tolerate it any longer and will offer this kind of prayers, as
recorded in Luke 18:7-8. it indicates, secondly, that God's judgment on the earth
is initiated by the saints' prayer.
