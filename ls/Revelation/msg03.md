the church as the testimony of Jesus.

the lampstands.

all golden in nature. all shining forth
light. in order to shine the lampstands
must have oil burning in the lamp. to shine
we must have oil.

all the churches/lampstands are identical.
the only differences are negative. it
is wrong to aspire to be different, but
neither should we copy the negative things.
it is not normal if a man who has seven
holes in his head aspires to have five just
to be different.
