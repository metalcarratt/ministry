the phrase "in Jesus" is not used in
the Bible as much as "in Christ".
especially this: "the tribulation and
endurance and kingdom in Jesus." In
Jesus denotes the suffering and
peraecuted Jesus.

throughout His time on the earth,
the Lord was especially persecuted
by religion. He was put to death by
the Jewish religion. as those who
are fellow partakers with Him, we
also suffer persecution from religion.
