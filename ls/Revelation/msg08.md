The lampstands

First seen in exodus 25, later in zechariah 4, finally in revelation 1. 

Gold symbolises the father in His nature. The form symbolises Christ the Son as the embodiment of God. The lamps signify the Spirit as the expression. This is a picture of the Triune God. Furthermore, in this picture the Father and the Son are both one, since the nature and embodiment of the Triune God is essentially One. However in expression, the Triune God is first sevenfold in the individual type of the lampstand (Christ) and later 49 times in the type of the churches as the seven lampstands.

In zechariah the seven lamps are the seven eyes of the stone, and the seven eyes of God. In revelation the seven eyes of the Lamb are the seven eyes of God and the seven Spirits. In exodus the focus is on Christ as the lampstand, in Zechariah the Spirit as the lamps shining, on Revelation, the duplication of the expression.

- the lampstand is for Gods building. Often we cannot see light until we come to the church
