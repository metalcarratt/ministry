2nd failure - miriam and aaron rebelling against moses.

moses married a cushite woman but God said nothing about this, indicating He must
not have had a problem with it. miriam and aaron took advantage of this to accuse
moses and say that God had also spoken to them. however Moses was God's deputy authority
so it was a serious thing for them to touch him. the germs of ambition within them
needed to be rooted out.

moses was meek and did not defend himself. eventually God Himself came to vindicate
moses, saying that he was one to whom God spoke face to face, not in obscure words,
and who beheld His form. He punished miriam with leprosy.
