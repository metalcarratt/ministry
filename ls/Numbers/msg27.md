concerning the tithe of the children
of Israel (10%), which became the
Levites portion, aince they had no
allotment of land but their portion
was God alone. What was offered to
God as a tithe was further tithed by
the Levites. so the 10% of the 10%
became the Levites offering to the
Lord. this became a heave offering.

the tabernacle with the ark was at
the center of the children of Israel,
and all the work of the Levites was
to handle the tabernacle, the
furnishings of the tabernacle, and
the altar, all of which are types
of Christ. in our human life our
focua should be handling Christ. in
our jobs we are not working for ourselves
but for Christ. this will become
a heave offering, a type of the
ascended Christ.
