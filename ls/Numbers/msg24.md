God's threefold judgment of the
rebellion of God's people:
- against Korah and his companions was the judgment of the earth to swallow them up
- against the 250 leaders was the judgment of fire from heaven to consume them.
- against the rest of the people, who continued to murmur waa the judgment of the plague which issued out of them

the censors of the 250 were beaten into silver
platings to cover the altar as a reminder
of the result of rebellion.

even after the first two rebellions the
people still murmured, showing how 
incurable is the rebellion in fallen
man.

God may forgive but sometimes He forgives
through judgment. we need to be fearful
and pray for the Lord's mercy.
