The blessing in Numbers 6 is not a material blessing but a promise of the
Triune God to give Himself to those who have dealt with defilement, have passed
the test of jealosy, and who have taken the vow of the nazarite.

Of the Father:
- to keep us
- according to John 17:11 this is to be kept in His name, that is, in His dispensing Person
- according to John 17:15, this is to be kept from the evil one.

Of the Son:
- His face (presence) shining on us
- Being grace continually to us

Of the Spirit:
- Lifing up His countenance upon us; to approve of us, assure us, promise us, give Himself ro ua
- Giving us peace
