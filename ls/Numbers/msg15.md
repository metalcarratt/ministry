the passover is a type of the Lord's table. it has a number of statutes and ordinances:
- those who are unclean tbrough contacting death, or are on a journey, still need to keep it (at a separate time).
- eaten with unleavened bread (eliminate all the sinful things)
- with bitter herbs (regret and repentance, with a bitter sense regarding sins)
- not to be left to the morning (enjoyed in a full way)
- no bones to be broken (Christ as the divine, eternal, unbreakable, indestrucitble life)
- strangers permitted to eat it (the gentiles)

it is related to God's move

> We should not think that partaking of the Lord's Table has nothing to do with God's move
> or with our move with God in His activity. The Lord's table has much to do with God's move
> on earth. Therefore we must keep the Lord's table.

- a new start
