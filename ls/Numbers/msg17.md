in numbers 9 God's leading is through the cloud and the trumpets. in numbers 10
God's leading is directly through the ark, which set out before all the camp
of Israel, travelling even up to three days ahead, searching a resting place for
His people. the ark is the incarnated Christ.

the cloud and the trumpets are for God's guidance, but the Ark is for God's leading.
guidance is general, leading is specific. he cut the way, but not paved the way. the path
He took through the wilderness was rugged, not paved. but Christ has cut the way,
leading us on. we cannot trust in anyone except Him alone (moses tried to trust
his father in law but could not).

number 10:35 is quoted in psalm 68:1, and psalm 68:18 is quoted in Ephesians 4:8.
by putting these verses together we see the ark that went before them was Christ,
who went into death and rose out of death and entered into ascension. number 10
finishes with Moses' cry for the Lord to return, which can be prophetically
interpreted as the Lord's second coming.
