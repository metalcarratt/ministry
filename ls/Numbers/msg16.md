God's leading.

God's leading comes from the cloud. the Spirit is the consummated Spirit. the cloud
is also the presence of the Lord. the cloud is related to rain and rain is related
to water. when the cloud becomes rain water comes to the earth. thus the cloud is
related to water. we can say that the Lord's leading is to where there is water. if the
place we are leaves us feeling dry then we are in the wrong place. we need to go before
the Lord to seek His presence. often we are dry because we have done something wrong,
but we may not know what we have done wrong. so we need to 'grope', for example, by
confessing our sins over different matters and from different angles until we sense
we have touched something. this is the Lord's leading.
