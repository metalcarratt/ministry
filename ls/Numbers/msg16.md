God's leading.

God's leading comes from the cloud. the Spirit is the consummated Spirit. the cloud
is also the presence of the Lord. the cloud is related to rain and rain is related
to water. when the cloud becomes rain water comes to the earth. thus the cloud is
related to water. we can say that the Lord's leading is to where there is water. if the
place we are leaves us feeling dry then we are in the wrong place. we need to go before
the Lord to seek His presence. often we are dry because we have done something wrong,
but we may not know what we have done wrong. so we need to 'grope', for example, by
confessing our sins over different matters and from different angles until we sense
we have touched something. this is the Lord's leading.


> Today we cannot see the Spirit, but we can see wbat issues from the Spirit. The issue is the sense within us that we are being watered. However, often as we move, we have the sense deep within that we are dry. When we are dry, we should not move, but neither should we stay. This dryness is a strong sign that we are wrong with God, either in our position or in other matters. Hence, we need to pray, not in a general way, but by confessing. Often we do not know in what we are wrong. Since our dryness is a sign that we are wrong and therefore do not know what to confess, we need to grope. In our groping, we may confess from different angles concerning our mistakes, shortcomings, wrongdoings, defects, and even fleshly activities and lustful desires. We may need to confess from many different directions. We need to confess by groping until our feeling in our prayer reaches something, and the cloud is there. Then we know tbe Lord's leading
