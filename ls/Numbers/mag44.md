the distribution of the spoil of
attacking the midianites was done
peacefully without any opinion
because of the one accord. in
distributing the spoils there were
multiple categories: those who went
to fight, those who stayed back (and
probably prayed), a portion for God
taken from those who went and given
to eliezer the high priest, a portion
given to the levites from those who
stayed. 

in addition to this, some officers
brought a free will offering to the
Lord of their spoil, because not one
of their men had been lost in the
battle.
