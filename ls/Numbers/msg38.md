#### the conditon: not to marry outside the tribe of their father

the condition to receiving our inheritance today is to maintain the proper relationship in life. Today
our "tribe" is Christ and the church.

the further statute: if a man died without sons the inheritance would go to his daughters. If without
daughters it would go to his brother. If without brothers to his father's brother. If without father's
brothers, to the next closest of kin. This shows our possession of Chrst must be based on our relationship
in life.

the degree of our enjoyment of Christ is based on the degree of our relationship in life.

#### applied to us today

Christ is the good land. The church is our family. We have the right to enjoy the inheritance but to execute
this right we need the house and the right channels. we need to have the right relationship (fellowship) with
the church.

the enjoyment of Christ is also related to our fruit. if we claim to have an enjoyment but do not have a harvest (fruit-bearing),
that enjoyment may be wrong or unreal. A proper harvest is proof of our enjoymennt of Christ.
