the children of israel complained
of not having water and moses made
a big mistake in misrepresenting
God.

in Gods eyes it was His responsibility
to feed and water His people. thus He
was not angry with them and was happy
to provide water for them. thus He
told moses to speak to the rock so
that water would flow out. when they
lusted after quails He was angry, for
that was a lust out of the flesh. but
this time was something for their
necessity.

Moses was angry when God wasnt angry
and thus misrepresented God. He did
not sanctify God, that is, make Him
holy, that is, distinct from the
heathen gods. the heathen gods could
be angry with anything but even in
His anger God was still merciful. for
example in judging Korah, even one of
Korah's sona became a priest.

Moses called them rebels but God
called his action as rebellion, and
because of this, he could not enter
into the good land.

we need to learn the lesson of not
going to God to accuse His people.
we need to forgive and forget.
