Overview of Numbers:

God's people formed into an army:
- according to age (maturity in life)
- as one Body
- journeying with God
- having the presence of God, the leading of the Angel of God, to live, walk and move in God
- passing through 42 stations of trials and sufferings in the wilderness to enter the good land
- fighting the enemies of God, Arad, the Amorites and tve Midianites, to gain the entrance to the good land
- bearing God's testimony:
  - the tablets of the testimony being a portrait of what God is
  - the Ark of the testimony being Christ as the center of God's people
  - the tabernacle of the testimony being God's people built up to be His dwelling place

in the eyes of the nations the children of Israel were wasting their time for forty years.
those in the world also think that we are wasting our time in the church life. but we
are happy to be those who use our time to care for and bear God's testimony.
