failures of gods people - lusting for egyptian food, becoming bored with manna

the complaining was by the "mixed multitude". through this judgment God purified
His people.

they grew bored of manna. we may also grow bored of eating Christ as our daily food
and wish to go back to the worldly diet.

God gave them quails to eat till they became sick of them then judged them in His
anger.

God appointed 70 elders, who prophesied when His Spirit fell on them. here is the
classic quote from Moses, that He wished all God's people were prophets. this word
is fulfilled in 1 corinthians 14, and will be fulfilled as part of God's NT economy.
