after the tabernacle was built and the army was formed, God now had a way
to live and move on earth through His people.

the tabernacle, altar, and all its furnishings, were anointed with oil. this is
the Spirit of God, without which nothing we have or do is related to God. but
through the anointing of the Spirit of God, we have become identified with God.

six wagons and twelve oxens offered for the moving of the tabernacle. the almighty
God limited Himself to travelling in wagons pulled by oxen. if the Lord is slow
in returning it is not because of any fault on His end, but because He has limited
Himself to us as His vessel.

> Why has the Lord not come yet? It seems that He is coming very slowly.
> Actually it is not the One in the wagon who is slow, the oxen are slow.
> This indicates the transportation we offer Him is slow.
