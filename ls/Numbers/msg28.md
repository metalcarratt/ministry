after the rebellion of korah and
his company, and the plague had gone
out, there was death wverywhere. in
order to restore the situation, God
gave the ordinance of the water
for impurity.

the composition of the water of
impurity was a red heifer, without
blemish, having no defect, having never
been under a yoke, slaughtered outside
the camp and the blood sprinkled
toward the tabernacle. this was
burned with cedar wood, hyssop and
scarlet, then added to living
water. when this water was sprinkled
on someone they were cleansed of the
impurity of death.
