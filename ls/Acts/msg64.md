concerning Paul's time in confinement.

Paul went to arabia shortly after hia conversion. at this time he likely considered:
- revelation through his knowledge of the old testament scriptures (for example allegorizing sarah amd hagar as law and grace in galatians)
- revelation received directly from the Lord (for example the operation of the four laws in romans 7-8)

in ceasarea he probably:
- considered the situation in jerusalem, especially the events in acts 15 and 21
- being burdened concerning God's NT economy, a matter he didnt't explicitly talk about until his latter books, esp Ephesians, 1 and 2 Timothy
