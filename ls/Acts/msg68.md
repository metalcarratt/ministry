Paul was appointed a minister and a witness. He was not appointed a preacher to preach
doctrines, but a witness, to testify of an experience.

The Lord told Paul to testify of the things in which He had appeared to Him, and in
which He would appear to Him. This shows that the contents of the visions Paul saw
was Christ. Every vision he saw and enlightenment he receieved were centered on Christ.
Thus what he testified was just what he saw of Christ.

All genuine enlightenment and vision is of Christ. if we have some supposed light but
it does not center on Christ, that light is vain. John in seeing revelation also saw
Christ. For example he saw Christ as the One walking in the midst of the lampstands,
and as the One on the throne.
