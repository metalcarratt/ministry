# Life-study of Acts

Introduction
- [Message 01](./msg01.md) - Acts a continuation of Luke, showing the living and ministry of the Lord in ascension
- [Message 02](./msg02.md) - In ascension Christ is very active, standing while Stephen was being stoned, working to gain Paul; He has all authority and power

The preparation
- [Message 03](./msg03.md) - Training His disciples to know His invisible presence ("appearing to them through a period of forty days" | Acts 1a)
- [Message 04](./msg04.md) - Speaking to His disciples about the kingdom of God to complete a dispensational transfer (Acts 1a)
- [Message 05](./msg05.md) - The Lord's public ascension to strengthen His disciples' faith; their persevering with one accord in prayer (Acts 1b)
- [Message 06](./msg06.md) - The pouring out of the economical Spirit as a fulfillment of the feast of the harvest (Acts 2a)
- [Message 07](./msg07.md) - The difference between the essential and economical Spirit (Acts 2a)
- [Message 08](./msg08.md) - Speaking in tongues (Acts 2a)
- [Message 09](./msg09.md) - Calling on the name of the Lord to enjoy our salvation (Acts 2:21)
- [Message 10](./msg10.md) - Peter's message: concerning Christ's death, resurrection and ascension (Acts 2)
- [Message 11](./msg11.md) - Peter's message: emphasising the Lord's humanity; instructing the people to repent, be baptised, receive forgiveness, receive the Spirit
