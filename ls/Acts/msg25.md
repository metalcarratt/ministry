Saul's conversion
- having a hebrew, greek and roman background
- being trained in the law, a hebrew of hebrews
- saved by a light from heaven, revealed as "Jesus, whom you persecute". Saul's persecuting the believers was his persecuting Jesus, the corporate "Me"
- needing a member to be identified with the Body.
- Spirit baptism accompanying identification with the Body, indicating that the power of the economical Spirit is directly related to the Body
- having water baptism as an outward confirmation
