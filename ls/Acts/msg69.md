continuation of paul's message to agrippa. the contents of the lord's commission to him:
- to open people's eyes
- to turn them from darkness to light and from the autbority of satan to God (in God's NT jubilee this is the freedom of the slaves)
- the forgiveness of sins
- to receive an inheritance, the all-inclusive Christ (in the jubilee, to receive back our possession, our inheritance)
- with all those who have been sanctified by faith, which is to be transformed
